How It Started

Founded in 2007 by Jeetendra Bamboli, Weben began as a small office chair manufacturing unit. A pivotal moment came 15 years ago when a suggestion to use wicker for chairs inspired a game-changing shift to outdoor furniture. Driven by this vision, Jeetendra collaborated with architects and designers, leading to innovative projects like patented wicker baskets for modular kitchens. Within two years, the product gained nationwide success, marking the beginning of Weben’s journey to becoming a leader in premium outdoor furniture.

Today at Weben

Today, Weben Outdoor is an Indian company specialised in designing and manufacture of Outdoor furniture with proven excellence. Presently, Weben Outdoor offers an ever expanding range of outdoor furniture solutions customised to suit every need for hospitality, recreational and discerning private patrons.

Premium Materials We Use

Rehau Wicker: Premium Durability

Weben Outdoor Furniture features Rehau Wicker, made from Raucord, a premium synthetic weaving material known for its superior durability and weather resistance. Manufactured with German engineering, Raucord is UV-resistant, temperature-proof, and designed to withstand the elements for 10-12 years. It’s eco-friendly, easy to clean, and maintenance-free, ensuring a carefree outdoor experience. Available in multiple colors and patterns, it’s the perfect blend of innovation and style for both outdoor and indoor furniture.

UV Rays Resistant Cushion Fabric

UV Rays Resistant Cushion Fabric offers a wide selection of colors and textures, complemented by durable outdoor-resistant braided ropes and straps. Featuring top-quality fabrics from Agora (Spain) and Sunbrella (USA), paired with Quick Dry Foam, these cushions are built to withstand the toughest weather challenges. Designed for superior strength, easy care, and long-lasting durability, they resist fading and degradation from sunlight exposure, ensuring a fresh, vibrant look for years. Backed by a 5-year warranty, our cushions provide both comfort and resilience.

About Us

Weben Outdoor, a leading outdoor furniture brand, operates a 5000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art manufacturing unit in Pisoli, Pune. Combining advanced technology, creative design, and expert craftsmanship, our 30 skilled artisans specialize in metal bending, welding, weaving, carpentry, and cushion making. With high-quality materials like Jindal aluminium pipes and in-house woodwork, we ensure durable, non-corrosive, and aesthetically superior furniture. Our dedicated design and R&D teams consistently deliver innovative creations while maintaining stringent quality standards.
